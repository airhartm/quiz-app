The Fibonacci sequence is named after Italian mathematician Leonardo of Pisa, known as Fibonacci. He introduced the sequence to the West, although the sequence was previously known in Indian mathematics. The Fibonacci sequence consists of a string of integers; each succeeding integer is the sum of the previous two integers. Fibonacci numbers appear often in mathematics and biology.

In this simple game, the objective is to provide the missing numbers in a given Fibonacci sequence. Simply enter the correct integer in the provided blanks below. Then click Submit. Correct and incorrect responses will be recorded as green or red in the progress bar.
